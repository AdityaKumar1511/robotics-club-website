About Robotics Club NIT Patna

Innovating the future through robotics, AI, and intelligent systems

Our Vision

To lead the next industrial revolution by empowering students with cutting-edge robotic skills and an innovative mindset.

Our Mission

To provide high-end resources, expert mentorship, and a collaborative environment for solving real-world problems through automation.

Who We Are

The Robotics Club, NIT Patna is a student-driven technical society dedicated to advancing robotics, automation, artificial intelligence, and embedded systems. Since its inception, the club has served as a hub for innovation, research, and hands-on engineering.
