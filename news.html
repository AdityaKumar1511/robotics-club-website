Dec 20, 2025 Tech Insight

Waymo Robotaxis Stall During SF Outage

A widespread power outage in San Francisco led to Waymo robotaxis stalling at intersections, raising key questions about autonomous vehicles' crisis resilience. Traffic signal failures triggered system delays and highlighted gaps in teleoperation support, prompting calls for updated safety protocols and regulatory standards to ensure reliability in emergencies. California regulatory bodies are now investigating operational safeguards for autonomous fleets. Read More
